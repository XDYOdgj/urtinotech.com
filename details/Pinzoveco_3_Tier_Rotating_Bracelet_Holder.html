Pinzoveco 3 Tier Rotating Bracelet Holder

The Pinzoveco 3 tier rotating bracelet holder is a sleek and functional way to store and display your jewelry. This attractive jewelry storage solution is built with a sturdy wooden base for durability and stability, and the integrated design board is perfect for labeling, pricing, or adding a personal touch to your display.
