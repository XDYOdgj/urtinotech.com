Rotating Jewelry Display Stand

Elevate your jewelry presentation with this stylish and functional display stand. Designed for vendors and boutique owners, it offers seamless organization and a polished look to showcase your collection. The 360-degree rotating mechanism on six arms makes it easy to view and access necklaces, earrings, bracelets, keychains, and lanyards from any angle, ensuring every piece gets the attention it deserves.

Key Features

  • 360-degree rotating 6 arms for easy access to necklaces, earrings, and bracelets
  • Adjustable height range from 15 to 26 inches for versatile tabletop display use
  • Durable black metal construction with a stable base for long-lasting support
  • Scratch-resistant matte black finish enhances display elegance and durability
  • Quick setup with included wrench and instructions for instant use at events

Why Choose This Stand?

Whether you're setting up at a vendor event, craft show, or retail store, this stand is built to deliver both performance and style.
